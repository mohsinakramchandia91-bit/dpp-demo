# Digital Product Passport (DPP) Analytics Platform

## What It Is
An enterprise-grade Digital Product Passport (DPP) analytics and
verification platform designed for global supply chains.

The platform captures verified product events from QR scans and
buyer verification flows, transforming them into compliance-ready,
auditable datasets.

---

## Core Capabilities
- Product authenticity verification (PASS / FAIL)
- Public product passport access analytics
- Compliance confidence scoring
- Supply-chain transparency metrics

---

## Delivered As
- Snowflake Secure Data Share
- Analytics-ready SQL views
- Enterprise documentation & schema

---

## Who Uses This
- Retail brands
- Importers & exporters
- Compliance teams
- Sustainability & ESG analysts

---

## Why It Matters
- Supports EU Digital Product Passport requirements
- Eliminates counterfeit & data tampering risks
- Enables audit-ready reporting
- Improves buyer trust & transparency

## Data Compliance
