# Digital Product Passport (DPP)

## Problem
• Counterfeit goods
• No trust after export
• EU DPP regulations coming

## Solution
• QR on every product
• Public verification page
• Tamper-proof product identity

## How It Works
1. Factory generates product ID
2. QR printed on packaging
3. Buyer scans → PASS / FAIL
4. Analytics logged automatically
