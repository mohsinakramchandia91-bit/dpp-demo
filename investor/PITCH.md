# Digital Product Passport (DPP)

## Problem
- EU regulations require traceable, verifiable product data
- Manufacturers lack simple compliance tools
- Paper-based audits are slow and costly

## Solution
DPP provides:
- QR-based public product verification
- Tamper-proof compliance logs
- Export-ready audit reports (EU aligned)

## Market

- Textile exporters (Pakistan, Bangladesh, India)
- Electronics & consumer goods manufacturers
